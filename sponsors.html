Sponsors

State Farm

Jennifer O'Brian's State Farm office in Smithtown is part of a global insurance company. Jennifer's branch is deeply committed to supporting the local community, particularly through partnerships with various non-profit organizations across Long Island. These collaborations address a wide range of needs, from supporting animal welfare to providing essential resources for people in need. One of the core values of State Farm is fostering a strong community connection.

Chocolate Panda

Chocolate Panda is a local Long Island DJ company that provides music entertainment services for various events, from weddings and corporate functions to private parties and school dances. Chocolate Panda is known for their versatile music selection, covering genres from R&B to electronic dance music. In addition to their DJ services, they also release their own music on all major platforms, including Apple Music and Spotify. Chocolate Panda regularly supports local events and festivals, contributing to the vibrant Long Island music scene.

Stony Brook Starbucks

Starbucks is a global coffeehouse chain renowned for its high-quality coffee, diverse beverage options, and welcoming atmosphere. Beyond serving millions of customers daily, Starbucks in Stony Brook actively engages in community support, particularly through partnerships with local Long Island non-profit organizations. The company is committed to promoting a positive social impact, and our local shop's dedication to helping animals further reinforces the company's core value of community connection.

Delaware Consulting Club

The Delaware Consulting Club at the University of Delaware is a student organization focused on developing consulting skills and preparing members for careers in consulting and business. Through case studies, workshops, and networking events, the club provides resources for students interested in problem-solving, strategy, and professional development in the consulting industry.

Fairfield Investment Banking Society

The Fairfield Investment Banking Society is a student-led organization at Fairfield University dedicated to educating and preparing members for careers in investment banking and related financial fields. The society offers workshops, guest lectures, and networking events to enhance students' understanding of financial markets, valuation techniques, and industry trends. By fostering a collaborative environment, the society aims to equip members with the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed in the competitive world of investment banking.

UDress Magazine

UDress Magazine is a student-run fashion and lifestyle publication at the University of Delaware, established in 2005. With over 150 members from more than 24 different majors, it offers students practical experience in creating a professional magazine. UDress publishes biannual issues, each accompanied by events like the Fall Fashion Event and Spring Launch, featuring runway shows and performances. The magazine covers topics ranging from fashion and beauty to culture and social issues, aiming to inspire self-expression and inclusivity.

Blue Hen Data Analytics Club

The Blue Hen Data Analytics Club at the University of Delaware bridges theoretical knowledge and practical application in data analysis and business intelligence. Members engage in hands-on projects, develop critical thinking skills, and utilize tools like Python and Power BI to derive real-world insights. The club collaborates with industry experts and alumni, fostering a community adept at analyzing data and translating it into effective business strategies.

Animal Love Club

The Animal Love Club at Fairfield University is a student-led organization dedicated to supporting and learning about animals. The club engages in various activities, including fundraisers, trips, guest speaker events, and projects, all centered around assisting different types of animals. It's an excellent platform for students passionate about animal welfare or those aspiring to become veterinarians.

Fairfield Business Analytics Group

The Fairfield Business Analytics Group is a student-led organization at Fairfield University dedicated to fostering interest and proficiency in business analytics among its members. The group offers workshops, guest lectures, and hands-on projects to enhance students' analytical skills and understanding of data-driven decision-making in business contexts. By collaborating with faculty and industry professionals, the group provides valuable networking opportunities and practical experience, preparing students for careers in the rapidly evolving field of business analytics.

Fairfield Startup Club

The Fairfield StartUp Club at Fairfield University is a student-led organization dedicated to fostering entrepreneurial skills and innovation among its members. The club offers workshops, guest lectures, and networking events to help students develop and launch their own business ventures. A key initiative is the annual Fairfield StartUp Showcase, a "Shark Tank"-style competition where students pitch their business ideas to investors for seed funding. In 2024, the 12th annual Showcase featured five teams presenting their innovative ideas to an investor panel in front of a live audience. The club also provides access to mentorship from alumni entrepreneurs and local business partners, supporting students in refining their business plans and navigating the startup landscape.

IMPRVE

IMPRVE is a clothing brand dedicated to individuals who go the extra mile. Their product line includes a variety of apparel designed to support and enhance performance. For instance, compression clothing has been shown to improve athletic performance, accelerate post-workout recovery, and enhance circulation. By focusing on quality and functionality, IMPRVE aims to provide attire that meets the demands of active lifestyles.

Fairfield Founders

Fairfield Founders is an independent 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization led by Fairfield University alumni. It operates as a pre-seed accelerator program dedicated to supporting early-stage startups within the Fairfield ecosystem. The organization offers mentorship, strategic guidance, and essential resources through a collaborative network of alumni, faculty, and community members. By fostering community-engaged entrepreneurship, Fairfield Founders aims to drive business success and innovation.

Music Production Club - University of Delaware

The Music Production Club (MPC) at the University of Delaware is a student organization that connects music producers and enthusiasts interested in music production. The club fosters a collaborative community where students can record, produce music, learn from each other, and receive feedback. As of Spring 2024, MPC holds meetings on Mondays from 8-9 PM in Purnell Hall, Room 229. To join or learn more, you can reach out via their Instagram handle, @ud_mpc.

Fairfield Investments

Fairfield Investments, LLC is a New York-based financial services firm specializing in project finance, green energy, and sustainable industry investments. Led by Jim Peet, the company manages projects exceeding $1 billion in value, offering customized financial solutions, SPVs, and government-backed support to enhance project viability.
